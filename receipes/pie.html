Description

Not too many people would know this spaghetti squash pie is not actually made with coconut. I wasn't sure what to do with a bumper crop of spaghetti squash, and this quickly became an annual answer to a now non-issue!

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
  2. Beat sugar and eggs together in a large bowl until light and frothy. Beat in melted butter, lemon juice, and vanilla until well blended. Stir in spaghetti squash.
  3. Pour mixture into baked pie shell. Dust the top with nutmeg and cinnamon.
